1. Introduction

GAB2XYZ Pte. Ltd. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Gabet mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address - Required for account creation and authentication
  • Password - Stored in encrypted/hashed form; we cannot access your actual password
  • User ID - A unique identifier assigned to your account

2.2 Profile Information

  • Display name - Optional name shown in the App
  • Avatar - Profile picture or selected avatar
  • Preferences - Currency, timezone, and locale settings

2.3 Financial Information

  • Transactions - Amount, merchant name, date, description, and category of your financial transactions
  • Categories - Custom spending categories you create
  • Recurring transactions - Templates for repeated transactions
  • Budgets - Budget limits and spending targets you set

We do not collect or store your bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or login credentials for financial institutions.

2.4 User Content

  • Chat messages - Messages you send within the App
  • Receipt photos - Images of receipts you upload
  • Profile pictures - Photos you upload for your profile

Your Responsibility: It is your responsibility to remove any personally identifiable information (such as names, addresses, phone numbers, or other sensitive data) from content before sharing it with the App. We do not automatically detect, hide, or remove such information. You have full control over what you share.

2.5 Group Information

If you use group features:

  • Group memberships - Groups you create or join
  • Member information - Email addresses of users you invite
  • Shared transactions - Transactions shared within groups

2.6 Technical Information

  • Device tokens - Used solely for delivering push notifications
  • Session data - Authentication tokens stored securely on your device

2.7 Subscription Information

  • Subscription status - Whether you have an active Gabet Plus subscription
  • Usage credits - Number of AI-powered message processing requests used

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core functionality - Track transactions, manage budgets, and organize your finances
  • Power AI features - Categorize transactions and extract receipt data
  • Deliver notifications - Send alerts about spending patterns, budget limits, and recurring transaction reminders
  • Enable collaboration - Allow you to share financial information with group members you invite
  • Process subscriptions - Manage your Gabet Plus subscription status
  • Provide customer support - Respond to your inquiries and troubleshoot issues

What We Do NOT Do With Your Data:

  • We do NOT sell your personal data to any third party
  • We do NOT use your data for marketing purposes
  • We do NOT use your personal data to improve our products (we may use anonymized, aggregated statistics)
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT track you across other apps or websites

4. Artificial Intelligence Processing

4.1 How AI Is Used

The App uses artificial intelligence to provide features such as:

  • Automatic transaction categorization
  • Receipt text extraction

4.2 Third-Party AI Services

To provide these features, certain data is processed by third-party AI service providers.

4.3 Data Shared With AI Providers

When you use AI-powered features, the following data may be sent to AI providers:

4.4 AI Provider Data Handling

AI providers process this data solely to generate responses and do not:

  • Store your data beyond the immediate processing request

  • Share your data with third parties

By using AI-powered features in the App, you consent to this data processing. You may choose not to use AI features, though this will limit App functionality.

6.1 Where Data Is Stored

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)

6.3 Your Responsibility

You are responsible for:
